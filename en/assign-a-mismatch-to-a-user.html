        Assign a mismatch to a user

        To assign a mismatch to a user for investigation:

        1. Open the reconciliation item.

        2. Fill in the Assignee field.

        3. Click Save.

        The user will see the assigned item in the Assigned to me section of the Reconciliation overview. They can investigate and fix it.
